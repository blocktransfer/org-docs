## Covered Sensitive Customer Information

Covered sensitive customer information means sensitive customer information that is within BlockTransfer's Regulation S-P scope because it is:

- customer information of a natural-person securityholder tied to an issuer for which BlockTransfer acts or has acted as transfer agent;
- in BlockTransfer's possession, custody, systems, workflows, files, exports, backups, email, or operational records; or
- received, maintained, processed, or otherwise accessible by a service provider on BlockTransfer's behalf.

## Use In Procedures

When a procedure asks whether customer information or sensitive customer information is involved, use this page as the standard definition source before deciding whether to escalate, contain, notify, dispose, retain, or review a vendor.
