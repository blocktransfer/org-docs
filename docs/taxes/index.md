# Tax Records

BlockTrans Syndicate uses its annual financial statements and supporting transaction records to prepare the applicable tax return and schedules. Filed returns are published here by tax year.

## Filing checklist

- **Form 1120-S:** U.S. income tax return for an S corporation.
- **Form 1125-A:** Cost of goods sold, when applicable.
- **Form 8949:** Sales and other dispositions of capital assets, when applicable.
- **Schedule K-1:** Shareholder reporting of income, deductions, and credits.

The checklist identifies forms tracked during preparation. The forms required in a particular year depend on that year's activity and the completed return.
